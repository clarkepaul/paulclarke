Experience product designer.
Father. DIY enthusiast.
Doer of creative things.

I'm a multidisciplinary digital product designer at Silverstripe.

I believe that human-centred design creates meaningful products and services that, when aligned with business goals, can make awesome things happen. I strive for that sweet spot between customer desirability (do they want it?), feasibility (can we deliver it?), and viability (will it work for our business?).

I've been a product designer at SilverStripe for over 10 years, leading a small UX team and the user experience and design of the Silverstripe open source CMS and Silverstripe's dedicated hosting and deployment service Silverstripe Cloud.

Over my career, my focus has ranged from designing print material like magazines, branding, marketing material, website design, and digital products including the front-end development of them.

Whether on my own or in collaboration with others, I love taking new ideas, setting them up with the foundations to be successful and encouraging them to grow.



Personal projects and activities
